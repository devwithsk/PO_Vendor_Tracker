# 📦 Automated Purchase Order & Vendor Tracker

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Automated Purchase Order & Vendor Tracker** is an Excel-based procurement and vendor management solution designed to help businesses monitor purchase orders, track vendor deliveries, and measure supplier performance from a centralized system.

Procurement teams often struggle to manage multiple purchase orders across spreadsheets, emails, and manual follow-ups. This project automates PO tracking, identifies overdue deliveries, calculates vendor on-time delivery performance, and provides a structured view of procurement activities.

By consolidating procurement data into a single system, businesses can improve vendor accountability, reduce delays, and gain better visibility into purchasing operations.

---

## 🚀 Business Problem

Procurement departments handle numerous purchase orders and vendor deliveries every month. When order tracking is managed manually, it becomes difficult to maintain visibility over pending orders and supplier commitments.

### Key Challenges

- Purchase orders scattered across multiple files
- Difficulty tracking delivery timelines
- Delayed vendor follow-ups
- Lack of visibility into pending orders
- No standardized vendor performance measurement
- Increased risk of procurement delays

Without a centralized tracking system, businesses may face inventory shortages, delayed operations, and inefficient supplier management.

---

## 💡 Solution

To solve these challenges, I developed an **Automated Purchase Order & Vendor Tracker** in Microsoft Excel.

The solution automatically:

- Tracks purchase orders and delivery status
- Identifies overdue deliveries
- Flags delayed vendor shipments
- Calculates vendor on-time delivery rates
- Monitors pending and completed orders
- Consolidates procurement information into a single reporting system

This enables procurement teams to manage supplier performance proactively and improve operational efficiency.

## 🔄 Workflow

### Step 1: Record Purchase Orders

Enter purchase order details including vendor information and expected delivery dates.

### Step 2: Track Delivery Status

Monitor shipment progress and update delivery records.

### Step 3: Validate Delivery Timelines

Compare actual delivery dates with expected delivery dates.

### Step 4: Identify Delays

Automatically flag overdue and delayed deliveries.

### Step 5: Measure Vendor Performance

Calculate on-time delivery rates for each supplier.
